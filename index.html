How to play:

Use your arrow-keys to slide the tiles.
Two tiles with the same value in line can be merged.
The goal is to merge the tiles and get the 2048 tile.

The score is a sum of the merged tiles, and best is the highest score you have ever gotten.

Press the info button again to close this pop-up.
